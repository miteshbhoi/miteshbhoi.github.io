WHAT IS I NEED A RAND?
 
It is a legal crowdfunding system that enables it's community members to to achieve their objectives that require serious financial backing. The community will consist of one million members who will be actively donating on a daily basis. Yes! On a daily basis!
 
Read More
 
 
 
 
How does it works?
 
 
 
 
User Registers
 
 
 
 
User gets Authenticated and Approved
 
 
 
 
User broadcasts what they desire to achieve
 
 
 
 
The community is notified
 
 
 
 
Donation is made
 
 
 
 
 
HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE TO GET FUNDED?
 
 
There isn't a specified timeline but you will get funded provided you are an active donor.
 
 
 
 
WHO IS THE MONEY PAID TO?
 
 
The money goes directly to your account.
 
 
 
 
WHAT'S IN IT FOR US?
 
 
Like all businesses, for us to survive we will need a small percentage for every succeful donation made.
 
 
 
 
IS THIS LEGAL / REGULATED?
 
 
We are in a process of registering with the South African FSB and talks have already begun. Please note that this method of pooling money is new in South African and hasn't been regulated by the FSB. So we are working on it and feedback on the progress will be sent to those who have subscribed.
 
 
 
 
HOW MUCH AM I EXPECTED TO DONATE ON A DAILY BASIS?
 
 
Between R1 and R2 and nothing more. So we advise that you put away R730 per annum.
 
 
 
 
HOW DO I SEND SUCH A SMALL AMOUNT TO A RECEPIENT?
 
 
We have tested by transfering R2.00 from one Capitec account to the next using cellphone banking and it worked. We are yet to test other banks. But for now, Capitec works and it is instant.
 
 
 
 
HOW MANY TIMES MAY I ASK TO BE FUNDED?
 
 
You are only allowed three requests per annum. No matter how big or small.
 
 
 
 
HOW WILL INEEDARAND KNOW THAT I HAVE PAID?
 
 
There is an option to upload a picture of the receipt or proof of payment inside every user account. So you will be using this daily.
 
 
 
 
IS THERE A JOINING FEE?
 
 
Yes. We need R59 to set you up with an awesome account and all other cool stuff that comes with it for your convenience.
 
 
 
 
DO I GET ANY COMMISSION FOR REFERRALS?
 
 
Yes! R100 for every succesful registration.
 
 
 
 
DO YOU HAVE AN APP?
 
 
We are currently in the process of developing one but rest assured that you will be notified when both mobile apps for IOS and android have gone live.
